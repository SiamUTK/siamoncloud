### 5. **Hotel & Accommodation Booking**

- **What to Sell**: Hotel reservations, vacation rentals, hostels
- **Revenue Model**: 10-15% commission from hotels OR markup on rates
- **Integration**: Connect to Booking.com API, Expedia, Agoda

### 6. **Travel Insurance**

- **What to Sell**: Travel insurance policies (medical, cancellation, baggage)
- **Revenue Model**: 20-40% commission from insurance partners
- **Partners**: Allianz, AXA, World Nomads
- **Why**: High-margin, low-effort passive income
- **Estimated Revenue**: $10-80 per policy

### 7. **Visa Processing Services**

- **What to Sell**: Visa applications, document verification, expedited processing
- **Revenue Model**: Service fee per application ($50-300)
- **Target**: International travelers to Thailand, Thai travelers abroad
- **Why**: Complex process that customers will pay for convenience
- **Estimated Revenue**: $100-500 per visa

### 8. **Airport Transfer & Transportation**

- **What to Sell**: Private cars, limousines, VIP meet & greet
- **Revenue Model**: 15-25% commission OR fixed markup
- **Partners**: Local transport companies, Grab, Bolt
- **Why**: Essential service, easy to bundle with bookings
- **Estimated Revenue**: $20-100 per transfer

### 9. **Tours & Activities Marketplace**

- **What to Sell**: Day tours, cooking classes, temple tours, adventure activities
- **Revenue Model**: 20-30% commission from tour operators
- **Integration**: GetYourGuide API, Viator API, local operators

### 10. **Travel SIM Cards & eSIM**

- **Revenue Model**: Markup 30-50% on wholesale price
- **Partners**: Airalo, Truphone, local carriers

- **Estimated Revenue**: $10-50 per SIM
